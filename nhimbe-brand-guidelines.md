## Introduction

**Nhimbe** (pronounced /ˈnhimbɛ/) is the traditional Shona practice of communal work where community members come together to help each other with large tasks—harvesting, building, celebrations. The concept embodies Ubuntu: collective effort for shared benefit.

As a product, Nhimbe is the gatherings and events platform within the Mukoko ecosystem, replacing the generic "Mukoko Events" naming with an authentically African identity that reflects our community-driven mission.

-----

## Brand Positioning

### Tagline

> *"Together we gather, together we grow"*

### Brand Voice

**Inviting, communal, celebratory**

Communications should feel warm and welcoming, emphasizing togetherness and shared experiences. Nhimbe celebrates community moments—from intimate gatherings to large cultural celebrations.

### Parent Brand Relationship

Nhimbe is a **Mukoko product** with a hybrid distribution model:

- **Web:** Standalone presence at **nhimbe.com** (with nhimbe.africa as alternate)
- **Mobile:** Integrated within the **Mukoko Super App**

Always include the endorsement *"A Mukoko Product"* or *"Part of the Mukoko family"* in marketing materials and footer areas.

## Color Palette

Nhimbe uses the **Five African Minerals** palette with **Malachite** as the primary color, reflecting growth, nature, and gathering spaces.

|Role         |Mineral  |Light Mode|Dark Mode|
|-------------|---------|----------|---------|
|**Primary**  |Malachite|`#004D40` |`#64FFDA`|
|**Secondary**|Tanzanite|`#4B0082` |`#B388FF`|
|**Accent**   |Gold     |`#5D4037` |`#FFD740`|

### Color Usage

- **Malachite:** Primary actions, headers, key UI elements
- **Tanzanite:** Secondary elements, links to Mukoko ecosystem
- **Gold:** Highlights, rewards, celebratory moments

All colors achieve **WCAG AAA (7:1+)** contrast in both light and dark modes.

## Icon & Imagery

### App Icon

|Property  |Value                                       |

|Background|Malachite (`#64FFDA` dark / `#004D40` light)|

The `Users` icon represents community gathering—multiple people coming together, which aligns with the Nhimbe philosophy.

### Icon System

Nhimbe uses **profile-based icons** from Lucide:

|Icon     |Lucide Name   |Usage              |
|---------|--------------|-------------------|
|Community|`Users`       |Gatherings, groups |
|Profile  |`User`        |Individual attendee|
|Calendar |`CalendarDays`|Events, scheduling |
|Location |`MapPin`      |Venues, places     |
|Ticket   |`Ticket`      |RSVPs, ticketing   |
|Share    |`Share2`      |Sharing events     |

### Photography Style

Imagery should reflect:

- **Authentic African gatherings** — real moments, not staged
- **Celebrations and community** — warmth, connection, joy
- **Diversity** — representing the breadth of African communities
- **Natural settings** — outdoor gatherings, cultural spaces

Avoid stock photography that feels generic or Western-centric.

## Ubuntu Philosophy Integration

Nhimbe embodies the Ubuntu principle: *"Ndiri nekuti tiri"* — I am because we are.

The traditional nhimbe practice aligns perfectly with our open source philosophy:

- **Collective effort for shared benefit** — like open source contributions
- **Community-organized, not top-down** — decentralized planning
- **Everyone contributes, everyone benefits** — shared ownership
- **Strengthening community bonds** — through shared experiences

## Usage Examples

### Correct Usage

✅ "Join us at the nhimbe gathering this Saturday"
✅ "nhimbe — A Mukoko Product"
✅ "Discover events on nhimbe.com"
✅ Using Malachite as the primary action color

### Incorrect Usage

❌ "Join us at the Nhimbe gathering" (capitalize)
❌ "NHIMBE" (all caps)
❌ Using hexagon patterns (use profile icons instead)
❌ Using Tanzanite as primary (that's Mukoko's primary)
